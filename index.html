About Me

Hi there, my name is Shailendra Sekhar.

I am a passionate AI researcher and engineer, driven by the challenge of transforming cutting-edge artificial intelligence into real-world solutions. My work spans deep learning, reinforcement learning, robotics, and intelligent systems, with a strong focus on building AI-driven models that enhance automation, decision-making, and human-machine collaboration. Currently, at iHub-Data, IIIT Hyderabad, I am developing assistive technologies that integrate brain-computer interfaces (BCIs) with robotic systems. My research revolves around translating neural signals into real-time robotic control, creating innovative solutions that push the boundaries of AI-driven accessibility in healthcare. Beyond technical problem-solving, I thrive on tackling complex, interdisciplinary challenges. My background in AI, embedded systems, and decision sciences allows me to design scalable and interpretable models that bridge the gap between theoretical advancements and practical applications. I am particularly passionate about explainability in AI, ensuring that reinforcement learning and autonomous decision-making systems are not just powerful but also transparent and robust. Whether through intelligent automation, predictive modeling, or robotics, my goal is to create AI that is not only innovative but also ethical, reliable, and deeply integrated into human-centered applications.
